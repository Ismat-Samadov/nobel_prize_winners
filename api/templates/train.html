Active Learning Strategies
Uncertainty Sampling

Selects samples where the model is most uncertain about its predictions. Good for finding edge cases and improving decision boundaries.

Query by Committee

Uses multiple models to identify samples with highest disagreement. Effective for robust model training.

Diversity Sampling

Ensures selected samples are diverse and representative of the data distribution. Good for coverage.

Hybrid Sampling

Combines uncertainty and diversity for balanced sample selection. Often provides the best overall performance.

Demo Mode

This demo uses synthetic data for training simulation. In a real scenario, you would upload your own CoNLL format training data. The system will guide you through the annotation process using your chosen active learning strategy.
